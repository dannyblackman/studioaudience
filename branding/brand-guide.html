Clear space

Maintain minimum clear space around the wordmark equal to the cap-height of the "s" in "studio." This protects legibility and presence in all contexts — don't let other elements crowd it.

04 Voice & Tone
Smart but
never cold
We lead with curiosity and deliver with clarity. Warm, approachable, jargon-free — because good research should be understood by everyone.
Curious
We ask the right questions. Our tone conveys genuine interest in understanding, not just collecting data.
What's really driving churn?
Our data shows a 12% increase in attrition metrics...
Clear
No jargon, no fluff. Complex findings translated into language anyone in the room can act on.
Users drop off because shipping costs surprise them.
Leveraging cross-functional synergies in the user journey...
Confident
We stand behind our insights. Assured without arrogance, opinionated without rigidity.
The data is clear: your onboarding needs rethinking.
It might possibly be worth considering perhaps...
Lead with the insight
Don't bury the finding. Start with what matters, then give context.
Show the human
Behind every data point is a person. Keep them visible.
Be specific
Replace vague claims with concrete examples. Numbers need stories.
Respect their time
Every word earns its place. If you can say it shorter, do.
