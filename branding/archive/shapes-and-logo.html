02 — Logomark
S & A Letterforms
Each built from the same circles and elongated ovals. Thick rounded forms with circular terminals — like the Steerwise approach.
S — Connected
Continuous thick strokes with rounded joints. Fluid and confident.
S — Three Pills
Three staggered ovals. The S emerges from the rhythm of the offset.
S — Flowing
Thick rounded S curve with prominent circular terminals at each end.
A — Pill Frame
Vertical + horizontal ovals. Dark frame, orange crossbar. Strong and open.
A — Angular
Two thick diagonal strokes with rounded caps. Orange crossbar. Architectural.
A — Round Top
Circular arch with pill legs and crossbar. Soft but structured.
SA — Lockup
S (three pills) + A (pill frame) side by side. Clean monogram for favicon, icon, stamp.
sa — Lowercase
Thick rounded lowercase forms. The s is pure stroke, the a is a circle + stem.
03 — Concept Direction
Connecting the Dots
Qual research is literally about connecting dots — finding patterns across conversations, linking insights to outcomes. The mark IS that process.
Dots — S Path
Dots traced through an S path. Varying opacity = the insight emerging from noise.
Three Connected
Most dots are grey noise. Three light up and connect — the insight found in the data.
Grid — Pattern Found
A grid of data points. Some light up and connect — the pattern in the noise.
Constellation
Five points, connected. An abstract constellation — the insight map. Two key dots in orange.
Three Dots
As simple as it gets. Three data points, two connections, one insight (orange). Iconic.
Linked
Three dots joined by an oval. Circles + ovals working together. The dots are the audience, the oval is the connection.
